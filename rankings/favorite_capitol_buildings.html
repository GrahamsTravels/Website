Ranking The Capitol Buildings I Saw

Personal Ranking of Capital Buildings *did not see Korea or Singapore

4) New Zealand (Wellington)

Called the beehive, it’s fine, but nothing too special.

3) Japan (Tokyo)

Nice looking building, don’t let you get too close though.

2) Vietnam (Hanoi)

Feels very Vietnamese with the trees growing out of it, unlike most of the surrounding architecture in the government district which are old French colonial buildings.

1) Australia (Canberra)

Very cool design being built into a hill top. Shame the surrounding city is an ugly bore.
